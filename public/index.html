About Us

JKCIT Solutions is a Software Technology Training cum Development Company situated in Hyderabad. It's management and teaching faculty includes either working IT professionals/expertise in IT industry.
All of them are highly experienced in designing/development of software solutions in their respective domains.
We are most popular for our training approach that enable students to gain real-time exposure on cutting-edge technologies. The training is completely industry oriented and interview oriented. During the initial days of training itself,All the concepts will be taught with realtime examples and in an easily understandable way. Currently we operate as three strategic business units focusing on IT Training, IT Services and IT Staffing. Our technological expertise, high quality standards, creativity and efficiency are combined in our services to deliver maximum value to our customers.
